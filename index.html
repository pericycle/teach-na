Description

First course in numerical methods. Topics include: finding roots of nonlinear equations, polynomial interpolation, solution of linear systems of equations, numerical differentiation and integration, and solution of initial value problems for ordinary differential equations.

Textbooks
  • Numerical Analysis, Pearson, 3rd edition 2019, by Timothy Sauer.
  • A First Course in Numerical Methods, SIAM 2011, by Uri M. Ascher and Chen Grief.
  • Numerical Mathematics & Computing, Cengage Learning, 7th edition 2012, Ward Cheney and David Kincaid.
  • Numerical Analysis, Cengage Learning, 10th edition 2015, Richard L. Burden, J. Douglas Faires, & Annette M. Burden.
